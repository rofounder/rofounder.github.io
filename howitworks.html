How-It-Works!

Activating Tag
Action Another action Something else here
  • Register Your Account
  • Verify your email address
  • Sign-In to view your Dashboard
  • Enter 12 digit uniquie ID in the Activate Tag field
  • Click on Activate     
  • Enter your Asset Information
    and click on Save
  • Your Tag will now be activated
  • You can view Tag information in
    your Manage Tags Table
Returning Found Item
  • Enter the 12 digit unique ID on Homepage
    and click on Return Found Item
  • You will be prompted to enter your basic
    contact information for the asset owner
    to contact you
  • If the Owner has generated a Lost Item Alert
    Details of Owner will be visible for
    you to contact them 
  • On successfully returning the Lost Asset,
    You will be rewarded by the owner and Rofound
Sending Lost Item Alert
  • Click on Send Lost Alert Button
  • You will be prompted to Sign-In 
  • In the Dashboard, in Manage Tags table
    click on the Send Alert button corresponding
    to the Lost Asset      
  • Confirm your contact information,
    enter your Current Location,
    Reward Description and Message         
  • You will be notified by email and message
    when your asset is found
Lost Item Retrieval
  • When your Asset is found, you will be
    notified via email and message 
  • Also, you can view finder details in
    Manage Tags section
  • You can contact the finder via phone call,
    text message or email 
  • Please reward the finder for returning
    your lost item
  • If you don't wish to directly contact
    the finder, Rofound can retrive the 
    asset and deliver it to you               
  • You can contact the Rofound Support team
    for the same

Tag Design

Why Rofound Tags?

Secure Assets

Rofound StickTags and KeyTags drastically reduce the risk of permanently losing your valuable assets by providing a medium to return your lost assets

Theft Deterrent

Our StickTags & KeyTags unique ID code allows law enforcement agencies to identify you as the rightful owner of your lost/stolen item

Privacy 

Your personal information stays confidential, when a finder reports Item Found, we email you instantly with their details allowing you to contact the finder as per your convenience

No Batteries

Rofound Tags are tough, light, weatherproof, waterproof and maintenance free forever

No Subscription

Your Rofound Tags are registered for lifetime. There are no complicated subscriptions

No App Needed

Rofound Tags work on any smartphone or computer with a web browser. Simply enter the 12-digit Unique ID to report found item
