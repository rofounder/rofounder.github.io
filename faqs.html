Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Rofound StickTags work?
Rofound StickTags provides a secure online lost and found item recovery service. If you have lost your asset with Rofound StickTags and someone reports it found, we will notify you so you can arrange to have your item securely returned. We will also offer the finder a reward for his or her good deed. Everybody wins!
Why would anyone want to return my lost items to me?
It is pleasantly surprising to see how many honest people there are. Most of the time lost items are not returned to their rightful owner because the finder does not know who the item belongs to. That’s where Rofound StickTags comes in, we securely arrange to get your lost item back to you safely through our online lost and found service at www.rofound.com
Can you guarantee that if anyone finds my tagged Asset, that I will get them back?
No. Unfortunately we cannot guarantee a successful recovery every time an item is lost. While we expect that if someone finds your lost item they will report it to us most of the time, we simply cannot guarantee that this will happen every time
Will The tag rescue lost and found service work when I am out of India?
Absolutely. The Rofound Lost and Found online service will work for you wherever you may be and even while you are travelling outside of India. Provided that the item has an activated tag attached and that you have access to your emails. Rofound will notify you via email and/or message when your item is found
Why is Rofound service better than me just putting my personal details on my valuable items?
It is important that you protect your privacy from strangers. The Rofound unique IDs protects your personal information ensuring that lost items are recovered securely. We never provide a finder with your personal details unless you have generated a Lost Item Alert
What should I do in case I lose my product?
You must meet the finder OR pay a recovery fee + shipping charges for Rofound to retrieve and deliver your item to you
What should I do in case I lose my asset?
We encourage you to immediately generate a "Lost Item Alert" with as much information that can be provided from your end. We also encourage you to supplement our Finder Reward program
What if I am unable to generate Lost Item Alert immediately?
It is okay even if you don’t generate the alert immediately. The moment the finer reports Item Found, we will notify you via email and/or text message.
I have found an item with a Rofound Tag attached to it! How do I get it back to its rightful owner?
Thank you for doing the right thing. To ensure that the item gets returned to it’s rightful owner please go to rofound.com, and enter the 12 Digit unique ID and click on "Return Found Item"’ button. You will be prompted to enter your contact details for the owner to contact you. 

If the Owner has generated a Lost Item Alert details of Owner will be visible for you to contact them 


How do I get a reward for finding a Tag Rescue item and assisting getting it back to its rightful owner?
Thank you for doing the right thing. As soon as we have successfully notified our customer, we will be in contact with you. Rofound will reward you for your good deed as soon as the owner has the item back safe and sound.
I recently reported that I had found a lost item but I am not sure what I was meant to do next?
Once you have reported that you found a lost item, we will immediately notify our customer of the good news and put them in contact with you to arrange the safe return of their lost item. As soon as our customer has his item back safe and sound we will reward you for your good deed.
Is it possible for me to get Rofound branded with my company logo to use as promotional value added gifts?
Yes. Please contact us through our website for any requests of this nature.
How durable are your StickTags?
The StickTags are Vinly Laminated labels. They are 
1. Water Proof
2. Scratch Proof

3. UV Protected
The StickTags come with 3M™ adhesive to ensure firm grip with your valuable and prevents peeling.

Will the StickTags spoil my asset?
The StickTags comes with 3M™ adhesive on the back. The adhesive is has proven reusults that the efficacy of the product will not be compromised.
What if my tag breaks or fades?
All Rofound products come with a one-year warranty. It is highly unlikely for the Rofound Tags to fade or break. But even incase of such events, Rofound will replace the Tags. For replacement of Tags please contact our support team
I can't activate my Rofound Tags!
  • If you are having trouble activating your tag/ID code please double check the tag ID# and make sure the one that was entered is correct. Here are a few commonly confused letters/numbers:
    • The letter O and the number 0
    • The letter i/I and the letter l/L
    • The letter E and the number 3
    • The letter A and the number 4
    • The letter B and the number 8
    • The letter Z and the number 2
    • The letter S and the number 5

If still the problem persists, please get in touch with our support team.
